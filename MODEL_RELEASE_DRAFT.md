# Visual Era Model Release Draft

This is a practical first draft for review, not legal advice.

## Model Release

I, the undersigned, grant to Visual Era, its affiliates, contractors, licensees, successors, and assigns the irrevocable right to photograph, film, record, reproduce, publish, distribute, display, edit, adapt, promote, advertise, and otherwise use my name, professional name, image, likeness, voice, appearance, persona, biographical information, and performance in connection with content creation, promotion, marketing, publication, distribution, and related business activities.

I understand and agree that this release applies to still images, video, audio, livestream recordings, edited materials, excerpts, derivative works, promotional assets, and any other media now known or later developed.

I authorize Visual Era to use these materials worldwide, in perpetuity unless otherwise terminated in writing under a separate agreement, in all media and formats, including websites, social platforms, digital advertising, email marketing, sales materials, press materials, and internal business operations.

I understand that Visual Era may edit, crop, retouch, combine, adapt, translate, subtitle, dub, or otherwise modify the materials so long as the use is not unlawful or materially misleading.

I acknowledge that, except as otherwise stated in a separate written compensation or management agreement, I am not entitled to additional compensation, royalties, residuals, or approval rights for uses authorized by this release.

I represent and warrant that:

- I am at least 18 years old and legally able to sign this release.
- I have the full right and authority to grant the permissions described here.
- My participation and the rights granted under this release do not violate any other agreement or legal obligation binding on me.

I release and discharge Visual Era and its affiliates, contractors, licensees, successors, and assigns from claims arising out of the authorized use of the released materials, including claims relating to privacy, publicity, defamation, or emotional distress, except for claims arising from willful misconduct or unlawful use.

I understand that this release is intended to work alongside any client agreement, management agreement, content release, or platform-specific agreement I sign during onboarding. If there is a direct conflict between this release and another written agreement signed by both parties, the more specific provision governing the particular use will control.
